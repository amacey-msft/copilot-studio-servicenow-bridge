# Teams app manifest icons

Drop two PNG icons here before zipping:

- `icon-color.png`   - 192x192, full-color
- `icon-outline.png` - 32x32, transparent background, single-color outline

These are placeholders so `build.ps1` can zip the folder. Replace with your
own branded artwork before publishing to anyone outside the dev tenant.

See the Teams icon guidelines:
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/platform/concepts/build-and-test/apps-package#app-icons
